▸ QUERY 06
Lobar pneumonia with homogeneous consolidation and air bronchograms is the classic presentation of which pathogen?
Explanation: Streptococcus pneumoniae is the most common cause of community-acquired lobar pneumonia. Classic finding: homogeneous consolidation of an entire lobe with visible air bronchograms on CXR.
